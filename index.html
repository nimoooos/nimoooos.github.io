About

Lightning Labs offers 25ID a mission driven approach to Innovation and Modernization. We provide Soldiers with a physical makerspace and digital platforms for creative design and collaboration aimed at problems identified by Soldiers in their individual mission sets and capability gaps identified by command. Lightning Labs also provides consolidated and ongoing support via access to small grants, patent agents, workshops, and connections to 25ID senior leaders and academic partners. As the Army's primary innovation hub for jungle and nearshore warfare, Lightning Labs fosters innovation at all echelons, from the lowest level of command and higher, ensuring our division's readiness for the challenges of tomorrow's USINDOPACOM theater. We believe that great ideas can come from anyone, anywhere, irrespective of rank or position.

Organizational Structure

  • Our Innovation Director reports to the 25ID Deputy Commanding General of Operations and is responsible for ensuring that ongoing Lightning Labs inititiatives directly reflect the modernization priorities established at the division level. The Deputy Director is responsible for the daily operations of Lightning Labs and is the primary point of contact for all stakeholders at the brigade level and below. Our Senior Enlisted NCOIC completes the Lightning Labs Command Team and provides solution-centric leadership and advice, representing all the enlisted Soldiers involved in the innovations process.
  • Our Software Dev Team engineers build custom full-stack web applications.
  • Our Materials and Manufacturing Team utilize 3D printing technologies and electrical engineering to rapidly produce and demonstrate prototypes.
